2. What TapOwner does (so the model is clear)

  • Free owner lookup. The agent opens a map of Texas, taps a property, and sees the owner of record (name + mailing address) — sourced from public county appraisal records via the State of Texas TxGIO StratMap program.
  • Paid contact “trace” (the data-resale piece). For 29¢, the agent unlocks that owner’s phone/email, supplied per-lookup by a licensed third-party provider (BatchData) that performs Do-Not-Call scrubbing. We resell that contact data to the subscriber for their own real-estate use.
  • AI-drafted outreach. The agent can generate a one-to-one email, sent from the agent’s own email account — never from our servers.
  • Mini-CRM. The agent saves owners with private notes and pipeline status.
  • “Farm” / Reverse Prospecting. The agent draws an area on the map, sees every owner inside it (capped at 500), filters by property attributes (beds, baths, pool, sqft), and can export a CSV. That CSV contains owner names + mailing addresses (public record) and — for parcels the agent already paid to trace — phone numbers and emails.
  • Pricing / billing. Prospector $9.99/mo or Closer $19.99/mo, 30-day trial. Account creation and billing happen on tapowner.com, not inside the app.

Deliberate risk-reduction choices (please confirm these are the right lines to hold)

  • No autodialer, no mass texting, no robocalls anywhere in the product.
  • Contact lookups are one at a time and billed per lookup; outreach emails are one-to-one from the agent’s own mailbox.
  • Do-Not-Call and litigation-risk flags are shown on every phone number and cannot be turned off.
  • Protected records (Texas Tax Code §25.025) are excluded from display.
  • We are not a consumer reporting agency; the ToS prohibits all FCRA-covered uses (tenant screening, employment, credit, insurance eligibility).

Attachment A — Terms of Service (current draft)

1. What TapOwner is

TapOwner is a subscription service for licensed real estate professionals. It displays property ownership information compiled from public county appraisal records, and — when you choose to purchase a lookup — contact information supplied by a licensed data provider. TapOwner is operated from Texas and currently covers Texas properties.

2. Your account and billing

Plans are billed monthly through Stripe. Trials convert to a paid subscription automatically unless canceled before the trial ends. Per-lookup contact charges (“traces”) are metered to your subscription — you are only charged when a lookup returns verified contact data. You can cancel anytime; access continues through the end of the paid period. Manage your plan at tapowner.com.

3. Acceptable use — the important part

By using TapOwner you agree to ALL of the following:

  1. Lawful use only. You will use TapOwner and any data obtained through it solely for lawful purposes connected to your real estate business.
  2. No FCRA uses. TapOwner is not a consumer reporting agency and its data is not a consumer report. You will not use it to determine eligibility for credit, insurance, employment, housing/tenant screening, or any other purpose covered by the Fair Credit Reporting Act.
  3. You own your outreach compliance. You are solely responsible for ensuring your calls, texts, and emails comply with all applicable laws — including the TCPA and federal/state Do-Not-Call rules for calls and texts, and CAN-SPAM for email. Do-Not-Call and litigation-risk flags shown in the app are informational aids, not legal advice, and displaying a number does not mean it is lawful for you to call it.
  4. Honor opt-outs. If a property owner asks you not to contact them, you will honor that request promptly and permanently.
  5. No harassment or stalking. You will not use TapOwner to harass, intimidate, stalk, or harm any person, or to locate a person for any purpose unrelated to a legitimate real estate inquiry.

We may suspend or terminate accounts that violate this section, without refund.

4. Data accuracy

Ownership records come from county appraisal districts and are updated on their schedule, not in real time. Contact data comes from a licensed third-party provider and is not guaranteed to be current or accurate. Verify independently before acting on it.

5. License and restrictions

Your subscription grants a personal, non-transferable license to use the data inside TapOwner for your own business. You may save individual contacts you have purchased. You may not resell, redistribute, scrape, bulk-export, or use the service to build or enrich any database or competing product.

6. Disclaimers and liability

The service is provided “as is” without warranties of any kind. To the maximum extent permitted by law, TapOwner’s total liability for any claim is limited to the amount you paid in the twelve months before the claim arose.

7. Changes and contact

We may update these terms; material changes will be announced by email or in the app. Questions: fred@salasers.com.

Attachment B — Privacy Policy (current draft)

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. Two kinds of people, two kinds of data

TapOwner handles data about subscribers (real estate professionals with accounts) and about property owners (whose ownership records appear in the product). This policy covers both.

2. What we collect from subscribers

  • Account: your email address, and the agent profile you choose to enter (name, brokerage, phone) used to sign your outreach emails.
  • Billing: handled by Stripe. We never see or store your card number.
  • Usage: product events (app opens, lookups purchased, emails drafted, properties saved) tied to your account, used to operate and improve the service.
  • Location: used on your device to center the map around you. Your GPS location is not stored on our servers.
  • Contacts: the app writes a contact to your phone only when you tap “Save to Contacts,” using the standard iOS contact form. We never read your address book.

3. Where property-owner data comes from

Ownership records (owner name, property and mailing address, assessed values) come from public records published by Texas county appraisal districts through the state’s StratMap program. Contact information (phone numbers, email addresses) is supplied per-lookup by a licensed data provider that performs Do-Not-Call scrubbing, and is purchased by a subscriber for their own business use.

4. Who we share data with

Only the processors needed to run the service:

  • Stripe — payments and subscriptions.
  • BatchData — the licensed contact-data provider (receives the property address you look up).
  • Anthropic — AI email drafting (receives the property details and your agent profile for the draft you request; their API does not train on this data).
  • Railway — cloud hosting for our servers and database.
  • Google — address search geocoding (receives the address text you type).

We do not sell subscriber data. We do not run ads.

5. Property owners: your choices

If you are a property owner and want your contact information suppressed from TapOwner lookups, email fred@salasers.com with the property address and we will suppress it and forward the request to our data provider. Ownership records themselves are public county records; corrections to those must be made with your county appraisal district.

6. Retention and security

Purchased contact results are cached for up to 90 days. Account data is kept while your account is active and deleted on verified request. Data is encrypted in transit, access is restricted, and we maintain a written information security program.

7. Contact

Privacy questions or deletion requests: fred@salasers.com. Material changes to this policy will be announced by email or in the app.
